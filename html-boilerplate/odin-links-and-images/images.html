JPEG Images

>

JPEG images are good for photos. They have high color gradients and extensive palettes but do not have transparent pixels. They tend to be in smaller file sizes than an equivalent PNG.

GIF Images

GIF images are animated but lack an extensive color palette.

PNG Images

PNG images are great for anyhting not photos or animated, like icons, diagrams, and logos.

SVG images

SVG images are vector images and can scale up or down without loss of quality. Great for resonsive design.
